# Sound Alert Files

Place your custom sound files here:

## Timer Sounds
- `cd2.wav` - Played when dropping from +3 to +2 time
- `cd1.wav` - Played when dropping from +2 to +1 time  
- `failed.wav` - Played when the key fails (over +1 time)

## Blacklist Sound
- `intruder.mp3` - Played when a blacklisted player is detected in your group

## Requirements
- File format: `.wav` (recommended)
- Sample rate: 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
- Bit depth: 16-bit or 24-bit
- Mono or Stereo

## Example Sound Sources
- Free sound effects: freesound.org, zapsplat.com
- Create your own using Audacity (free audio editor)
- Extract from games/media (check licensing)

## Tips
- Keep sounds short (1-3 seconds)
- Moderate volume - don't blast your ears!
- Test in Preview Mode first: `/kdt timer preview`

## Disable Sounds
If you don't want sound alerts:
1. Open Timer Settings (gear icon or `/kdt timer settings`)
2. Scroll to "Sound Alerts"
3. Uncheck "Enable Sound Alerts"
